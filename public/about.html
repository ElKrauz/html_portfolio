About Me

Jr. Web Developer

I am a self-employed freelance web developer based in Mexico City. For the past year, I have dedicated myself to building digital solutions and bringing ideas to life through code. Operating as a freelancer allows me to work independently, manage my own schedule, and collaborate directly with clients who need a strong online presence to grow.

My primary focus is designing and developing custom websites for micro-businesses and small local shops. I understand the unique challenges that small business owners face, so I build functional, user-friendly, and responsive sites that help them reach more customers. From local diners to independent boutiques, I tailor each project to match the specific identity and goals of the business.

Living and working in Mexico City gives me a vibrant perspective on the local market and the entrepreneurial spirit of its people. Over my first year in the industry, I have successfully combined my technical skills with a personalized approach to client service. I look forward to expanding my portfolio, mastering new technologies, and helping even more small businesses succeed in the digital world.
